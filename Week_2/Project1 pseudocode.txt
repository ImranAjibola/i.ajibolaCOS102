3. Display the total price to the customer.
4. Prompt the customer for payment information (e.g., credit card details).
5. Process the payment transaction.
6. If payment is successful:

7. If payment fails:
   7.1. Display an error message.
   7.2. Allow the customer to retry payment or choose a different payment method.
